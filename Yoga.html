Jessamyn Stanley

Jessamyn travels all over the world teaching yoga and advocating for body positivity. She has hosts her own podcast called 'Dear Jessamyn' and in 2017 published a book, 'Every Body Yoga'. As well as being an extremely talented yogi, we love her in particular because she encourages people to take up space and champions 'Yoga for all bodies'. Find her yoga classes online at The Underbelly.
